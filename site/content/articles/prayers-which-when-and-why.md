###

 Astrology 
Moti Haptan Yasht is to be recited for overall astrological benefits of increasing positive influence of good planets/stars and decreasing negative influence of negative planets/stars. 
 To increase the positive influence of: 
Sun Khorshed Nyāsh and Khorshed Yasht
Venus Tir Yasht
Jupiter Hormazd Yasht
Mercury Khordad Yasht, Tir Yasht,
Moon Māh Bakhtār Nyāsh
 To reduce negative influence of: 
Mars Behrãm Yasht
Saturn Āvã Yasht, Hom Yasht
Rahu & Ketu Hom Yasht, Haptan Yasht
